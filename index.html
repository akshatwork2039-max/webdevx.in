FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take? +

Static website: 3–5 working days. E-commerce: 7–10 days. Custom dynamic website: 2–4 weeks depending on scope and revisions.

Do you offer maintenance? +

Yes. Every plan includes 30 days free support. After that, monthly maintenance plans start at ₹1,999/month covering updates, backups, and security.

Can I upgrade my plan later? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Static to E-commerce or Dynamic at any time. Akshat handles the entire migration — no disruption to your existing site.

How do payments work? +

We take 50% advance to start and 50% before final launch. For larger projects we can arrange milestone-based payments for your comfort.

Do you work with clients outside India? +

Yes! Akshat works with clients worldwide. All communication happens over WhatsApp, email, or video call. Payment can be made via international transfer or Wise.
